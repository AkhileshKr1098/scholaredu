What is B.COM

A Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) is an undergraduate degree program offered by many universities worldwide, focusing on business-related subjects. It typically covers a wide range of topics such as accounting, finance, economics, marketing, management, and business law. B.Com programs aim to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of business principles and practices, preparing them for careers in various industries such as banking, finance, accounting, consulting, and entrepreneurship. Overall, a B.Com degree equips students with the knowledge, skills, and credentials needed to succeed in today's competitive business environment.
